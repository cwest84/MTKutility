Welcome to the new MTKutility

This app will only work with a GPS device has the MTK GPS chipset.

Thanks for choosing to use MTKutility.  This version represents a total rewrite of the app to overcome the barriers Android updates created for the previous version.  I need to extend a speacial thank you to Tomas Garcia for all the help he provided with this effort.  As a result we are able to provide a product with a wider range of proven performance on phone and gps logger devices.

Please note that the entire interface has changed.  The details of how this new app works are in the Help document.  The menu is now Android's Drawer layout - swipe from the left - or click the 3 bar block at the top left of the screen.  Please review the Help document before continuing with MTKutility.  Most of the app functions have changed.

Android continues to isolate apps so that they can't affect resources from other apps.  You no longer have a choice on when files are created.  They will be in what your phone defines as the download folder.  You will need to use your phone's folder/file app to relocate the files if space is a problem.

To use this app you need to pair your GPS logger in your phone’s Bluetooth settings.  Once you have your GPS device paired, you need to assign it to this app on the Home screen. The app will fail if you attempt to connect without having a GPS logger assigned.

The activities that appear in the menu are driven by your GPS logger capabilities. While the Help document shows all the activities, the menu you see after you connect will reflect your GPS device configuration. 

I created this app for personal use and I am not able to provide dedicated time to dealing with issues.  However, I will try to resolve issues in my spare time.  You can email adt.androidapps@gmail.com, or better still, use the Send App Log menu item and add your comments to the generated email.  That way I will also have a bit of information about your devices too.  The app has extensive execution tracing built in and I may be able to resolve the problem.
